# ReelRivals Product Guidelines

## 1. Tone and Voice
The tone and voice of ReelRivals should be **Playful & Engaging**. Our goal is to create a fun, light-hearted, and enjoyable experience. The language should be approachable and enthusiastic, as if talking with a fellow movie and awards enthusiast.

## 2. Visual Identity & Aesthetic
The visual identity of ReelRivals should be **Vibrant & Dynamic**. The design should convey excitement and interactivity through the use of bright colors, energetic animations, and bold typography. This aesthetic will help create a modern and exciting user experience.

## 3. Communication Style
Our communication with users for notifications, alerts, and in-app messages will be **Supportive & Encouraging**. We want to frame messages in a positive and helpful way, guiding users and celebrating their participation and successes within the app.

## 4. Community Engagement
ReelRivals will foster community through an **Interactive & Social** approach. We will integrate social features like forums, leaderboards, and sharing options to encourage user interaction and friendly competition. The goal is to build a connected and engaged user base.

## 5. Content Guidelines
The content presented in ReelRivals will be **Curated, Factual, Comprehensive, and Educational**. We will prioritize accurate, verified information about award shows and nominees, while also providing detailed background on the awards, nominees, and the film industry. AI-generated insights can be used to enhance learning and discovery, and should be clearly differentiated from factual data.
